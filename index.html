The Ghaziabad Story

Our Royal Heritage

Anand Sagar was founded on the basic principle of absolute culinary purity. Nestled within the heart of Nandgram, Ghaziabad, we began as a simple family kitchen aiming to serve genuine traditional Indian sweets without synthetic preserves.

Today, our brand stands synonymous with authentic fine dining, soft golden tandoori breads, and sweets that bring nostalgic celebration joy to households.
